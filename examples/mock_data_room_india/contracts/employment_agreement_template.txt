WHEREAS the Company desires to employ the Employee and the Employee desires to accept
such employment upon the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set
forth and for other good and valuable consideration, the parties agree as follows:

1. EMPLOYMENT

1.1 The Company hereby employs the Employee as "Senior Software Engineer" and the
    Employee accepts such employment.

1.2 The Employee shall report to the "Engineering Manager" or such other person as
    the Company may designate from time to time.

1.3 The Employee shall be based at Bengaluru office but may be required to travel or
    be relocated as per business requirements.

2. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT

2.1 The employment shall commence on 1st April 2024 and shall continue until
    terminated by either party as per the provisions of this Agreement.

2.2 The Employee shall be on probation for a period of 3 (three) months from the date
    of joining, which may be extended at the sole discretion of the Company.

3. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 The Employee shall:
    a) Perform duties related to software development, coding, testing, and deployment
    b) Work on projects assigned by the Company
    c) Collaborate with team members and contribute to product development
    d) Maintain coding standards and documentation
    e) Participate in code reviews and technical discussions
    f) Comply with all Company policies, procedures, and work schedules

3.2 The Employee shall devote full time, attention, and skill exclusively to the
    business of the Company during working hours.

3.3 The Employee shall not engage in any other employment or business during the
    term of this Agreement without prior written consent of the Company.

4. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS

4.1 SALARY:
    The Employee shall be paid a gross annual salary of ₹18,00,000 (Rupees Eighteen
    Lakhs Only) payable in 12 equal monthly installments of ₹1,50,000 (Rupees One
    Lakh Fifty Thousand Only).

4.2 SALARY STRUCTURE:
    Basic Salary:                           ₹ 75,000 per month
    House Rent Allowance (HRA):            ₹ 37,500 per month
    Special Allowance:                      ₹ 25,000 per month
    Performance Bonus (variable):           ₹ 12,500 per month
    -----------------------------------------------------------
    Gross Salary:                          ₹1,50,000 per month

4.3 DEDUCTIONS:
    - Employee Provident Fund (EPF): 12% of Basic
    - Professional Tax: As applicable
    - Income Tax (TDS): As per Income Tax Act, 1961

4.4 BENEFITS:
    a) Medical Insurance: Group health insurance coverage of ₹5,00,000
    b) Accidental Insurance: Coverage of ₹10,00,000
    c) Provident Fund: Company contribution of 12% of Basic Salary
    d) Gratuity: As per Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972
    e) Leave encashment as per Company policy

4.5 ESOP (Employee Stock Option Plan):
    The Employee shall be eligible to participate in the Company's ESOP scheme as
    per the terms and conditions of the scheme, subject to Board approval.
    Indicative allocation: 500 stock options

4.6 PERFORMANCE BONUS:
    The Employee shall be eligible for annual performance bonus up to 20% of annual
    gross salary, subject to achievement of individual and company performance targets.

5. WORKING HOURS AND LEAVE

5.1 WORKING HOURS:
    Normal working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday, with 1 hour lunch
    break. The Employee may be required to work additional hours as per project needs.

5.2 LEAVE ENTITLEMENT:
    a) Earned/Privilege Leave: 21 days per year
    b) Sick Leave: 7 days per year
    c) Casual Leave: 7 days per year
    d) Public Holidays: As per Karnataka State Government calendar
    e) Maternity/Paternity Leave: As per applicable laws

5.3 Leave accrual and encashment shall be as per Company's Leave Policy.

6. TERMINATION

6.1 TERMINATION BY EMPLOYEE:
    The Employee may terminate this Agreement by giving 60 (sixty) days' written
    notice or payment of 60 days' salary in lieu of notice.

6.2 TERMINATION BY COMPANY:
    The Company may terminate this Agreement by giving 60 (sixty) days' written
    notice or payment of 60 days' salary in lieu of notice.

6.3 TERMINATION DURING PROBATION:
    During the probation period, either party may terminate with 15 days' notice.

6.4 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE:
    The Company may terminate the Employee immediately without notice or payment in
    lieu of notice for:
    a) Misconduct, fraud, or dishonesty
    b) Breach of confidentiality or non-compete obligations
    c) Willful disobedience or insubordination
    d) Habitual absence or negligence
    e) Conviction of criminal offense involving moral turpitude

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The Employee acknowledges that during employment, they will have access to
    confidential and proprietary information including but not limited to:
    - Source code, algorithms, and technical designs
    - Business plans, strategies, and financial information
    - Customer data and contracts
    - Trade secrets and know-how

7.2 The Employee agrees to:
    a) Maintain strict confidentiality of all such information
    b) Not disclose to any third party without authorization
    c) Not use for personal benefit or benefit of others
    d) Return all confidential materials upon termination

7.3 This obligation shall survive termination of employment indefinitely.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 All inventions, discoveries, designs, software code, documentation, and other
    intellectual property created by the Employee during the course of employment
    shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

8.2 The Employee hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such intellectual
    property to the Company.

8.3 The Employee shall execute all documents necessary to perfect the Company's
    ownership rights.

9. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

9.1 During employment and for a period of 12 (twelve) months after termination, the
    Employee shall not:
    a) Engage in or be associated with any business competing with the Company
    b) Solicit or accept business from Company's clients/customers
    c) Solicit or induce Company's employees to leave employment
    d) Work for any competitor in similar capacity

9.2 Geographic restriction: India and countries where Company operates

10. CODE OF CONDUCT

10.1 The Employee shall:
     a) Comply with all Company policies including IT Usage, Anti-Harassment,
        and Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH)
     b) Maintain professional conduct and business ethics
     c) Avoid conflicts of interest
     d) Report any violations of law or Company policy

11. BACKGROUND VERIFICATION

11.1 Employment is subject to satisfactory verification of educational qualifications,
     employment history, and reference checks.

11.2 Any false information provided shall be grounds for immediate termination.

12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

12.1 ENTIRE AGREEMENT:
     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes
     all prior agreements or understandings.

12.2 AMENDMENTS:
     Any amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

12.3 GOVERNING LAW:
     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

12.4 JURISDICTION:
     Courts at Bengaluru shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

12.5 SEVERABILITY:
     If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in effect.

12.6 ASSIGNMENT:
     This Agreement cannot be assigned by the Employee without Company's consent.

13. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

The Employee acknowledges having read, understood, and agreed to all terms and conditions
of this Agreement and has received a copy of the same.
