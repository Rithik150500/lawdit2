III. OBJECTS CLAUSE

A. MAIN OBJECTS OF THE COMPANY:

1. To carry on the business of software development, computer programming, system integration, IT consulting, and technology solutions.

2. To develop, design, create, modify, maintain, and market computer software, applications, mobile applications, web applications, and cloud-based solutions.

3. To provide information technology enabled services (ITES), business process outsourcing (BPO), knowledge process outsourcing (KPO), and related services.

4. To engage in research and development of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, blockchain technology, and other emerging technologies.

5. To provide consulting services in the field of digital transformation, enterprise architecture, cybersecurity, and technology strategy.

B. ANCILLARY OBJECTS:

1. To acquire, purchase, or otherwise obtain licenses, copyrights, patents, trademarks, and other intellectual property rights.

2. To enter into partnerships, collaborations, joint ventures with other companies for software development and technology services.

3. To import and export software, hardware, and technology-related equipment and services.

4. To establish and maintain offices, branches, and development centers in India and abroad.

5. To invest surplus funds in securities, mutual funds, or other investment instruments as permitted by law.

6. To borrow or raise money by issue of debentures, bonds, or other securities.

7. To do all such other things as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of the above objects.

IV. LIABILITY CLAUSE

The liability of the members is limited to the amount, if any, unpaid on the shares held by them.
