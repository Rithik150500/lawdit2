PRELIMINARY

1. INTERPRETATION

In these Articles, unless the context otherwise requires:
- "The Act" means the Companies Act, 2013 including any statutory modification or re-enactment thereof
- "The Company" means TECHVISTA SOLUTIONS PRIVATE LIMITED
- "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Company
- "Director" means a Director of the Company
- "Registered Office" means the registered office of the Company

2. Table F of Schedule I of the Companies Act, 2013 shall apply to the Company except insofar as they are altered, modified, or excluded by these Articles.

SHARE CAPITAL AND SHARES

3. AUTHORIZED CAPITAL
The Authorized Share Capital of the Company is ₹10,00,00,000 divided into 10,00,000 Equity Shares of ₹100 each.

4. ISSUE OF SHARES
Subject to the provisions of the Act, shares may be issued by the Board on such terms and conditions as it thinks fit.

5. RIGHTS ATTACHED TO SHARES
Each equity share shall carry one vote per share and equal rights to dividends.

6. TRANSFER OF SHARES
(a) The instrument of transfer shall be in writing in Form SH-4 or as prescribed under the Act.
(b) No share shall be transferred to a person who is not a member so long as any member is willing to purchase the same at fair value.
(c) The Directors may refuse to register any transfer of shares without assigning any reason.

7. TRANSMISSION OF SHARES
On the death of a member, the survivor(s) or nominee(s) or legal representatives shall be the only persons recognized by the Company as having any title to the shares.

8. SHARE CERTIFICATES
Every member shall be entitled to a share certificate under the common seal of the Company.

GENERAL MEETINGS

9. ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING
The Company shall hold an Annual General Meeting within six months from the close of the financial year.

10. EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL MEETING
The Board may convene an Extraordinary General Meeting whenever it thinks fit.

11. NOTICE OF MEETINGS
(a) 21 days' notice shall be given for calling a general meeting
(b) Notice shall be sent to all members, directors, and auditors
(c) Notice shall specify the place, date, time, and agenda

12. QUORUM
Two members present in person shall constitute a quorum for general meetings.

13. VOTING RIGHTS
(a) On a show of hands, every member present shall have one vote
(b) On a poll, voting rights shall be in proportion to shares held
(c) No member shall vote if calls or dues are payable

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

14. NUMBER OF DIRECTORS
The number of Directors shall not be less than two (2) and not more than fifteen (15).

15. FIRST DIRECTORS

16. POWERS OF DIRECTORS
The Board shall have all powers to manage the business and affairs of the Company including:
(a) Power to make calls on shareholders
(b) Power to borrow money
(c) Power to invest surplus funds
(d) Power to appoint officers and employees
(e) Power to make contracts
(f) Power to open bank accounts

17. BOARD MEETINGS
(a) Board meetings shall be held at least once every quarter
(b) Notice of 7 days shall be given for Board meetings
(c) Quorum shall be one-third of total strength or two Directors, whichever is higher

18. CHAIRMAN
The Directors may elect a Chairman from among themselves. In absence of Chairman, members may choose any Director to preside.

19. MANAGING DIRECTOR
The Board may appoint one or more Managing Directors for such term and remuneration as it thinks fit.

ACCOUNTS AND AUDIT

20. BOOKS OF ACCOUNT
The Company shall keep proper books of account as required by the Act.

21. FINANCIAL YEAR
The financial year of the Company shall be from 1st April to 31st March unless otherwise determined by the Board.

22. APPOINTMENT OF AUDITORS
Auditors shall be appointed in accordance with the provisions of the Act.

23. AUDIT REPORT
The Auditors shall make a report to the members on the accounts examined by them.

DIVIDENDS

24. DECLARATION OF DIVIDEND
The Company may declare dividends in general meeting but not exceeding the amount recommended by the Board.

25. INTERIM DIVIDEND
The Board may declare interim dividends.

26. DIVIDEND TO BE PAID OUT OF PROFITS
No dividend shall be paid except out of profits of the Company.

NOTICES

27. SERVICE OF NOTICES
Any notice may be served by the Company by:
(a) Delivering personally
(b) Sending by registered post
(c) Sending by electronic mode to registered email

INDEMNITY

28. INDEMNITY TO DIRECTORS
Every Director, officer, or servant of the Company shall be indemnified against all losses or liabilities incurred in execution of their duties, except due to willful negligence or default.

WINDING UP

29. DISTRIBUTION IN WINDING UP
If the Company is wound up, surplus assets shall be distributed among members in proportion to capital paid up on shares held.

PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY

30. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
All Directors, officers, and employees shall maintain confidentiality of Company information and trade secrets.
