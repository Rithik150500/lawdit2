AGENDA AND RESOLUTIONS:

RESOLUTION NO. 1: APPROVAL OF MINUTES
RESOLVED THAT the minutes of the Board Meeting held on 15th December 2023 be and are hereby approved and confirmed.

RESOLUTION NO. 2: APPROVAL OF ANNUAL FINANCIAL STATEMENTS FOR FY 2023-24
RESOLVED THAT the audited financial statements for the financial year ended 31st March 2024, comprising:
- Balance Sheet as at 31st March 2024
- Statement of Profit & Loss for the year ended 31st March 2024
- Cash Flow Statement for the year ended 31st March 2024
- Notes to Financial Statements

as prepared by the management and audited by M/s. Srinivasan & Associates, Chartered Accountants, be and are hereby approved.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the Board notes the following financial highlights:
- Total Revenue: ₹45,50,00,000
- Net Profit: ₹8,75,00,000
- Total Assets: ₹35,60,00,000
- Net Worth: ₹22,40,00,000

RESOLUTION NO. 3: DECLARATION OF DIVIDEND
RESOLVED THAT a dividend of ₹15 per equity share (15% on face value of ₹100) amounting to ₹75,00,000 be declared for the financial year 2023-24, subject to approval of shareholders in the Annual General Meeting.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the dividend shall be paid within 30 days from the date of declaration at the AGM.

RESOLUTION NO. 4: APPOINTMENT OF NEW EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN (ESOP)
RESOLVED THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 62 of the Companies Act, 2013, the Company hereby approves the creation and implementation of "TechVista ESOP Scheme 2024" for grant of stock options to eligible employees.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT:
- Total number of options reserved: 50,000 options
- Each option convertible into 1 equity share of ₹100
- Exercise price: ₹500 per share
- Vesting period: 4 years with 1-year cliff
- Eligibility: All permanent employees with minimum 1 year service

RESOLUTION NO. 5: APPROVAL OF BORROWING LIMITS
RESOLVED THAT pursuant to Section 180(1)(c) of the Companies Act, 2013, the Board be and is hereby authorized to borrow funds up to ₹20,00,00,000 (Rupees Twenty Crores) from banks, financial institutions, or other lending sources.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the borrowing may be secured by mortgage/charge on the Company's assets, both present and future.
