3. CODE OF CONDUCT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All employees must:

3.1 PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT
    - Maintain professional behavior at all times
    - Treat colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect
    - Dress appropriately (business casual)
    - Arrive on time for meetings and commitments
    - Communicate clearly and courteously

3.2 ETHICAL STANDARDS
    - Act with honesty and integrity
    - Avoid conflicts of interest
    - Refuse bribes, kickbacks, or inappropriate gifts
    - Report unethical behavior through proper channels
    - Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

3.3 CONFIDENTIALITY
    - Protect company and client confidential information
    - Not disclose proprietary information to third parties
    - Return all confidential materials upon exit
    - Use company information only for business purposes

3.4 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
    - Respect company's IP rights
    - Assign all work product to company
    - Not use company IP for personal benefit
    - Report any IP infringement

3.5 CONFLICT OF INTEREST
    - Disclose potential conflicts to management
    - Not engage in competing business
    - Not use company resources for personal gain
    - Avoid situations compromising objectivity

4. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
    TechVista is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on:
    - Gender, religion, caste, race, or ethnicity
    - Age, disability, or marital status
    - Sexual orientation or gender identity
    - Pregnancy or maternity

4.2 PROBATION PERIOD
    - Duration: 3 months (extendable up to 6 months)
    - Confirmation subject to performance review
    - Notice period during probation: 15 days

4.3 NOTICE PERIOD
    - Post-probation: 60 days (or as per employment contract)
    - Notice can be waived with mutual consent
    - Buyout option: Payment of notice period salary

4.4 BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
    - Conducted for all new hires
    - Includes education, employment, criminal record checks
    - Employment subject to satisfactory verification

4.5 CONFIDENTIAL AGREEMENTS
    - All employees sign NDA and IP assignment agreements
    - Non-compete clause: 12 months post-employment
    - Non-solicitation of clients and employees

5. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 SALARY
    - Paid monthly by 7th of following month
    - Credited to employee's bank account
    - Salary slips available on HRMS portal

5.2 COMPONENTS
    - Basic Salary
    - House Rent Allowance (HRA)
    - Special Allowance
    - Performance Bonus (variable)

5.3 DEDUCTIONS
    - Provident Fund (PF): 12% of Basic
    - Employee State Insurance (ESI): 0.75% (if applicable)
    - Professional Tax: As per state regulations
    - Income Tax (TDS): As per IT Act, 1961

5.4 BENEFITS

    Medical Insurance:
    - Coverage: ₹5,00,000 per family
    - Covers: Self, spouse, 2 children, parents
    - Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals
    - Premium paid by company

    Accidental Insurance:
    - Coverage: ₹10,00,000
    - Company-paid

    Provident Fund (PF):
    - Employee: 12% of Basic
    - Employer: 12% of Basic
    - As per EPF Act, 1952

    Gratuity:
    - Payable after 5 years of service
    - As per Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972

    Employee Stock Options (ESOP):
    - Eligible after 1 year of service
    - Subject to Board approval
    - Vesting: 4 years with 1-year cliff

5.5 PERFORMANCE BONUS
    - Annual bonus up to 20% of gross salary
    - Based on individual and company performance
    - Paid in June each year

5.6 ANNUAL INCREMENTS
    - Performance-based salary revision
    - Effective from April each year
    - Average increment: 8-15%

6. LEAVE POLICIES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 EARNED/PRIVILEGE LEAVE (PL)
    - Entitlement: 21 days per year
    - Accrual: Monthly (1.75 days per month)
    - Carry forward: Maximum 30 days
    - Encashment: On resignation/retirement (max 30 days)

6.2 SICK LEAVE (SL)
    - Entitlement: 7 days per year
    - Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days
    - No carry forward
    - Not encashable

6.3 CASUAL LEAVE (CL)
    - Entitlement: 7 days per year
    - For unforeseen circumstances
    - No carry forward
    - Not encashable

6.4 MATERNITY LEAVE
    - Duration: 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act, 2017)
    - For first two children: 26 weeks
    - For third child onwards: 12 weeks
    - Full pay during maternity leave
    - Work from home option available post-leave

6.5 PATERNITY LEAVE
    - Duration: 15 days
    - To be availed within 6 months of child's birth
    - Full pay

6.6 BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
    - Duration: 5 days
    - On death of immediate family member
    - Additional leave can be taken from PL

6.7 PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
    - As per Karnataka State Government calendar
    - Approximately 12-14 days per year
    - List published annually

6.8 LEAVE APPLICATION
    - Apply through HRMS portal
    - Advance approval required
    - Emergency leave: Inform manager immediately

7. WORKING HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

7.1 STANDARD HOURS
    - Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
    - Lunch break: 1 hour
    - Weekly hours: 40 hours

7.2 FLEXIBLE WORKING
    - Core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (mandatory presence)
    - Flexibility: 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
    - Subject to team and project requirements

7.3 WORK FROM HOME (WFH)
    - Hybrid model: 3 days office, 2 days WFH (subject to approval)
    - Full remote for specific roles (pre-approved)
    - Equipment provided by company

7.4 OVERTIME
    - Compensatory off for work beyond 9 hours/day
    - Requires prior manager approval
    - Comp-off to be availed within 30 days

7.5 ATTENDANCE TRACKING
    - Biometric/RFID card swipe mandatory
    - HRMS portal for attendance records
    - Monthly attendance report available

7.6 LATE COMING
    - Grace period: 15 minutes (max 3 times/month)
    - Excessive late coming: Loss of pay/disciplinary action

7.7 UNAUTHORIZED ABSENCE
    - Loss of pay
    - Disciplinary action for repeated absences
    - Termination if absent for 3+ consecutive days without intimation

8. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

8.1 PERFORMANCE CYCLE
    - Annual cycle: April to March
    - Mid-year review: September
    - Annual appraisal: April

8.2 GOAL SETTING
    - SMART goals aligned with company objectives
    - Set at beginning of cycle with manager
    - Quarterly review and updates

8.3 PERFORMANCE RATINGS
    - Outstanding (O): Exceptional performance
    - Exceeds Expectations (EE): Above average
    - Meets Expectations (ME): Satisfactory
    - Needs Improvement (NI): Below expectations
    - Unsatisfactory (U): Far below expectations

8.4 360-DEGREE FEEDBACK
    - Feedback from manager, peers, and subordinates
    - Anonymous and constructive
    - Used for development planning

8.5 PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN (PIP)
    - For employees rated NI or U
    - Duration: 60-90 days
    - Regular monitoring and support
    - Continued poor performance: Termination

14. TERMINATION AND EXIT
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

14.1 RESIGNATION
     - Submit written resignation to manager and HR
     - Serve notice period (60 days or as per contract)
     - Exit interview mandatory
     - Full and final settlement within 45 days

14.2 TERMINATION
     Grounds for termination:
     - Misconduct or disciplinary issues
     - Poor performance (after PIP)
     - Breach of company policies
     - Fraudulent activities
     - Extended unauthorized absence

14.3 EXIT PROCESS
     - Knowledge transfer and handover
     - Return of company assets (laptop, ID card, access cards)
     - Exit clearance from all departments
     - No-dues certificate
     - Experience and relieving letter

14.4 FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT
     Includes:
     - Salary for notice period worked
     - Leave encashment
     - Bonus (prorata)
     - Gratuity (if applicable)
     - PF settlement (through EPFO)
